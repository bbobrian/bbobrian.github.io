Tools for LLR consultants to hide shipped items in Audrey and download Facebook comments for simple giveaway winner selection.

If you're using Chrome:
1) Make sure your Bookmarks Bar is on (Ctrl-Shift-B if it's not)
2) Drag each of the links below onto the Bookmarks Bar

Firefox:
1) Make sure your Bookmarks Toolbar is on. If it is not, right click an empty area in the title bar and check Bookmarks Toolbar.
2) Drag each of the links below onto the Bookmarks Toolbar.

Hide Shipped

Show Shipped

A note about the hide/show shipped buttons - If you hide your shipped invoices and then refresh the page, the shipped invoices will show again and you will have to click the button to hide them.

Download FB Comments

To download FB comments:
1) Click on the permalink for the post, which is the light gray timestamp right under the user name.
2) Click the button in your bookmarks toolbar.
3) It will automatically click "View previous comments" until all comments have been loaded. (This takes a few seconds. Let it run.)
4) Once all comments are loaded, it will automatically download a "comments.csv" file. You can view it in Excel, or just in Notepad.
